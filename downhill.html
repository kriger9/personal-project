Downhill

A double barrel bushing combo is the most common choice for this type of riding. However, when getting into downhill, you will soon find that riders can get very particular (and creative) with their bushing combos! When it comes to downhill the ultimate goal is to maintain speed and make it all the way down to the bottom of the hill safely, ready for another run.
